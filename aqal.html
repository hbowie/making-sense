12 AQAL

AQAL is an abbreviation for “All Quadrants, All Levels,' used by Ken Wilber and the Integral Community as a reminder to view the universe from all Four Quadrants and from all Developmental Levels. It's also used as shorthand for ”all quadrants, all levels, all lines, all states, all types." See the Integral Life website for more info, especially concerning lines, states and types.
